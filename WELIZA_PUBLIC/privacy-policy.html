Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Weliza Stitching Company ("Weliza", "we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through weliza.in, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an inquiry through our website or contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email, we may collect:

  • Your full name or company name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any message details or order requirements you provide
  • Whether you are a wedding mall representative, wholesale distributor, or individual client

We do not collect payment card details through this website, as transactions are arranged directly via phone, WhatsApp, or in person.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and provide quotes or order details
  • To process wholesale, retail, or custom stitching orders
  • To maintain GST-compliant billing records as required by Indian law
  • To communicate updates about your order via WhatsApp, phone, or email

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your details may be shared only with:

  • Our internal team for order fulfillment and billing
  • Government authorities where required by law (e.g. GST filings)
  • Service providers such as WhatsApp Business, strictly to facilitate communication with you

4. Data Retention

We retain inquiry and order information for as long as necessary to fulfill your order, meet accounting/GST obligations, and resolve any disputes.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us using the details below.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use basic analytics to understand visitor traffic. We do not use this data to identify individuals personally.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website links to third-party platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these platforms.
